About Us

Our team of scientists, clinicians, and scholars at the Neurospirituality Lab explores the complex relationship between the brain and spirituality, combining expertise in neuroscience, psychology, bioengineering, and theology to understand how spiritual experiences shape cognition, emotions, and well-being.

Dr. Michael Ferguson, PhD
Lab Director

Michael Ferguson, PhD, is a Harvard neuroscientist pioneering neurospirituality—the study of human spirituality through neuroscience. He directs the Neurospirituality Lab at Brigham and Women’s Hospital, coordinates wellness programming at Harvard College, and has received teaching honors for courses on neurospirituality, virtue science, and meditation.
